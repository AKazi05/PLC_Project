Tibalis Posterior Workouts

  • 1. Resistance Band Plantarflexion: Sit on the floor with your legs extended. Loop a resistance band around a fixed point and the ball of your foot. Push your foot downward against the resistance.     
  • 2. Seated Shin Stretch: Sit on your heels with your knees bent and your toes pointed. Gently press down on your knees.     
  • 3. Duck Walks: Start in a low squat position. Take small steps forward while keeping your feet turned slightly inward.
